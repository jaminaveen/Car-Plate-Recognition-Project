Today, there are many students who travel to foreign countries to pursue higher education. The application is intended to help students evaluate their chances of acceptance for graduate study in Computer Science at various American universities. Due to the increased number of applicants, universities manually check the applicants who should get an admit into their university. Taking these issues into consideration, our goal is to build an efficient machine learning algorithms to assist students and university.
Assisting student Model: It is a classification model, that will allow students on 2 fronts: - Student can get a prediction on how likely the university will provide them with admit or reject based on his or her application file - Students can also get the TOP 5 university in which he should apply to based on the best-suited university
Assisting University Model: - This could be used by the university to analyze the profile of the student and make decision-based on the classification if the student would get the admission or rejection
